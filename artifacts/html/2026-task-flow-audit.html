Expert Findings

PriorityFindingProduct impact
High Calculator results need a clearer "what now" bridge immediately after the score. Business owners can understand the score but may not move quickly to recommendations, methodology, or sector context.
High Mobile Explorer hides the sector-to-calculator path too far down the flow. Users can browse sectors but can miss the most valuable action: assessing their own business.
Medium Some secondary UI states are too low-contrast for a public civic product. Trust and accessibility suffer, especially for step indicators, skip link, and small utility labels.
Medium Calculator transitions are functional but not intentionally focused for keyboard/screen-reader users. The flow works, but completion can feel less polished and less accessible than the visual design suggests.
Medium Shared result decoding needs stricter validation. Hand-edited query strings should fail closed instead of carrying invalid percentages/tasks into the client state.

Implementation Direction

  • Keep the current institutional editorial aesthetic; refine rather than replace.
  • Add early result next-actions without changing formulas or source data.
  • Make Explorer mobile conversion visible near the ranked sector context.
  • Raise low-contrast utility text and disabled/stepper states.
  • Harden shared-result inputs at the route boundary.
